WONG HOI YING

Wong Hoi Ying is a Hong Kong-based photographer and visual artist. She is currently studying in the School of Creative Media at City University of Hong Kong. Her works are mainly focused on conceptual and narrative photography. She is interested in exploring the relationship between human and nature, and the interaction between human and the environment. She is also interested in the concept of time and memory. Her works are often poetic and dreamy, and she likes to create a surreal and mysterious atmosphere in her works.
